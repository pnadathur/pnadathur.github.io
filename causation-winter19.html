All course materials will be posted on this page. Please check back regularly. Readings can be downloaded by clicking the following link and entering the course password. If you do not have the password, please email me.

I recommend the following collection as a general resource for those interested in causation and recent semantic research in causation. You should be able to access any of the chapters electronically, via the library:

  • Copley, B. & F. Martin. 2014. Causation in Grammatical Structures. Oxford: Oxford University Press.

NB: Readings should be completed prior to the class for which they are assigned. Lectures and supplementary material will be in English. Reading responses completed in English will be assessed on content alone, and will NOT be penalized for any grammatical errors. I recommend completing your responses in English; however, if you strongly prefer to do them in German, please let me know in advance so that I can make arrangements for you to receive feedback.

NB: Readings should be completed prior to the class for which they are assigned. Assignments can be emailed to me or handed in on paper. Lectures and assignments will be given in English. Assignments completed in English will be assessed on content alone, and will NOT be penalized for any grammatical errors. I recommend completing assignments in English; however, if you strongly prefer to do them in German, please let me know in advance so that I can make arrangements for you to receive feedback.

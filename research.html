ongoing work

Causation, semantics of aspect and telicity
A causal approach to the mereological structure of telic predicates, extensional approaches to (non-)culminating aspects. Joint work with Hana Filip.
  • 2021, with Hana Filip. [Poster] Telicity, teleological modality, and (non-)culmination. WCCFL 39, University of Arizona, Apr 8-11. [Accompanying handout] [Paper pre-print]
  • 2021, with Hana Filip. [Poster/slides] (Non)culmination inferences and teleological modality in telic predicates. Linguistic Society of America 2021 Meeting, Jan 7-10.
Causal dependence relations and causative verbs
Causal modeling approaches to lexical semantics, semantics/pragmatics of periphrastic causatives. Joint work with Sven Lauer.
  • 2020, with Sven Lauer. Causal necessity, causal sufficiency, and the implications of causative verbs. Glossa 5, 49.1-37
  • 2019. Presentation (invited). Sufficiency causatives. Cognitive Science seminar, Stanford University. March 14.
  • 2018, with Sven Lauer. Sufficiency causatives. In revision, comments welcome.
